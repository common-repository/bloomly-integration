Bloomly plugin provides a simple and intuitive way to integrate with the entire Bloomly system.

== Description ==

Bloomly plugin is a simple way to integrate your site with the Bloomly platform.

Bloomly is a platform designed to help publishers improve every visitor session by delivering a smarter combination of ad placements, layouts, and content to users as they land on their website.

Bloomly is used by everyone from independent website owners to major brands. It works with any CMS, host, or configuration.

For more information on the Bloomly platform and how we can optimize your sites, visit us at [https://www.Bloomly.com/](https://www.Bloomly.com/)

== Installation ==

1. Download the Bloomly plugin from the wordpress plugin directory.
2. Apply for an account with Bloomly [here](https://www.bloomly.com/apply-now/).
3. A Bloomly team member will schedule a discovery session with you to learn more about you, your brand, and your vision for the future. We help understand your earnings, growth, and brand goals and start organizing a plan to make it happen.
4. We begin exclusively managing your ads and help find influencer opportunities that may exist for your blog, brand, or website. Meanwhile, our team of SEO, technology, and marketing experts dive deep into your site to help find ways to help you grow your audience and improve their experiences using the Bloomly community.
